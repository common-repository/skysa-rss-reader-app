Display interactive summaries from an RSS (or Atom) feed in a dynamic ajax window on your site; customizable RSS feed app button.

== Description ==

The Skysa RSS Reader is a simple RSS reader for your site. You can choose how many RSS feed items to display and how much text to show in the preview. You also have the option of linking to the RSS feed source articles or allowing the preview to be expanded right on your site.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zj1uGXX2xrk&hd=1
[More Skysa App plugins](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/tags/skysa-apps) -|||- [Skysa App Bar Integration plugin](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/skysa-official/)

== Installation ==

1. Install the Skysa RSS Reader App either via the WordPress.org plugin directory, or by uploading the files to your server (in the /wp-content/plugins/ directory).
2. Activate the plugin.

You can now manage your RSS Reader app settings from Skysa Bar Apps > RSS Reader on your WordPress admin menu.

> ####Optional
> For added style customization and display options, you can install the [Skysa App Bar Integration plugin](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/skysa-official/) and connect it to a Skysa account.
